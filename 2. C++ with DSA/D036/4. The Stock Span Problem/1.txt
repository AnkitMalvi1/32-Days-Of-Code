STOCK SPAN PROBLEM :

The span of the stock's price today is defined as the maximum number of consecutive days (starting from 
today and going backwards) for which the price of the stocks was less than or equal to today's price.
Find the span of the stocks for all the days.

Output:
Array:[100,80,60,70,60,75,85]
Ans :  [1, 1, 1, 2, 1, 4, 6]
